Popular Questions About Solar

A rooftop solar system uses solar photovoltaic (PV) panels installed on your roof to generate electricity for homes or businesses and reduce grid dependency.

Solar panels generate DC power from sunlight which an inverter converts to AC power. Excess energy can be exported to the grid through net metering.

Systems include grid-connected, grid-connected with battery backup, and off-grid systems depending on your power requirements.

Net metering allows surplus solar energy to be exported to the grid, helping reduce your electricity bill through unit adjustments.
